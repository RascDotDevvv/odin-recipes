Orange chicken

Description:

Begin by preparing the orange sauce. In a saucepan, combine fresh orange juice, soy sauce, rice vinegar, brown sugar, grated ginger, and minced garlic. Bring the mixture to a simmer, allowing it to thicken into a glossy, irresistible sauce. The combination of citrusy brightness, savory soy, and the warmth of ginger creates a perfect harmony for your chicken.

For the chicken, coat bite-sized pieces in a mixture of cornstarch and seasonings, ensuring a crispy exterior when cooked. Fry the chicken until golden brown and delicious. Once done, toss the crispy chicken in the vibrant orange sauce, coating each piece with the sweet and tangy glaze. Garnish with sesame seeds and green onions for a finishing touch. This orange chicken is a symphony of flavors, delivering a delightful balance that will have your taste buds dancing with joy.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Orange juice
  • Brown Sugar
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. In a saucepan, combine 1 cup fresh orange juice, 1/3 cup soy sauce, 1/4 cup rice vinegar, 1/4 cup brown sugar, 1 tablespoon grated ginger, and 2 cloves minced garlic.
  2. Coat 1.5 lbs of bite-sized chicken pieces in a mixture of 1 cup cornstarch, 1 teaspoon salt, and 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  3. Fry the chicken in vegetable oil until golden brown and crispy.
  4. Toss the crispy chicken in the prepared orange sauce, ensuring each piece is coated.
  5. Sprinkle sesame seeds and sliced green onions over the orange chicken for a finishing touch.
