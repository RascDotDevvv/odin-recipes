Pizza

Description:

Begin with the pizza dough. In a mixing bowl, combine flour, yeast, sugar, salt, and warm water. Knead the mixture until you have a smooth, elastic dough. Allow it to rise in a warm place until it doubles in size. Meanwhile, prepare the pizza sauce by sautéing garlic and onions in olive oil until golden. Add crushed tomatoes, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer the sauce until it thickens, filling your kitchen with a mouthwatering aroma.

Once the dough has risen, roll it out into your desired pizza shape. Spread a generous layer of the homemade sauce over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust. Top it with your favorite ingredients—mozzarella cheese, pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, or whatever your heart desires. Bake in a preheated oven until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and delicious. Slice it up and savor the perfect blend of flavors in your homemade pizza creation.

Ingredients

  • Mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • Pizza dough
  • Salt
  • Meat
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C)
  2. Prepare the Meat Sauce
  3. Roll out the pizza dough in your desired shape
  4. Prepare the Cheese Filling
  5. Bake in a pre-heated oven
