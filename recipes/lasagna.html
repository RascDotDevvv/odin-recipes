Lasagna bolognesa

Description:

Start by preparing the meat sauce. In a large skillet, sauté finely chopped onions and garlic in olive oil until golden brown. Add ground beef and cook until browned. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer the sauce for about 30 minutes to let the flavors meld.

Now, it's time to assemble. In a baking dish, layer the meat sauce, lasagna noodles, and cheese mixture. Repeat until you've used up all your ingredients, finishing with a generous layer of cheese on top. Bake in the oven until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the edges are golden brown. Let it rest for a few minutes before slicing into the perfect, gooey, and flavorful lasagna layers. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Meat
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C)
  2. Prepare the Meat Sauce
  3. Cook the Lasagna Noodles
  4. Prepare the Cheese Filling
  5. Assemble and bake
